The redesign and development of the Burns & Wilcox website was not only a responsive, mobile-first update of the company's site, but it laid the groundwork for updates spanning all of the Kaufman Financial Group's family of companies. The build features modular content with extensive custom taxonomy and posts to power the WordPress site.

Overview

  • WordPress
  • Custom Post Types and Taxonomies
  • Modular Content with Intuitive Administration
  • Sass
  • Gulp
  • Fully responsive
  • Customized Search and Filtering
