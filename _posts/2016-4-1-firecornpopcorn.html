Moving to a fully responsive, multipage site, the Firecorn Popcorn brand was looking to promote their product in a mobile-friendly way. Big imagery, a takeover menu utilizing CSS transforms and transition animation, and parallaxing panels round out this spicy update.Additional functionality for collapse/expand videos and content are built for future use. This build was launched on a Digital Ocean droplet instance and deployed with Git using Trellis and Bedrock for server parity.

Overview

  • WordPress
  • Isotope image collages
  • Custom grid system
  • Parallax
  • Trellis/Bedrock
  • Sass
  • Gulp
  • Fully responsive and content managed
  • Let's Encrypt SSL
