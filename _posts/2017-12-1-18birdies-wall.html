18Birdies was looking for a interactive tool to share its app usage data at the 2018 PGA Show conference. Projecting the 4k experience on a 9-screen touch wall allowed show-goers to touch an interactive SVG map to prompt associated animated data. The interactive logic split the screens into different regions, allowing multiple users to interact with different regions simultaneously.

Overview

  • 4k Resolution optimized across 9-screen Touch Wall display
  • Raphael-basd SVG maps
  • Custom data architecture
  • ES6 content templating
  • Contextual Counter Animations
  • Sass
  • Gulp
