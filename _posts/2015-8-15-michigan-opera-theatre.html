The Michigan Opera Theatre was looking for a redesign and platform change for their website. Working with our designer, we wanted to showcase the beautiful images and bring a modern, flat design to the Detroit culutral instituion.

Ease of updates was paramount to this highly automated custom theme, sharing elements across sections to maintain consistency and ease of management.

Overview

  • WordPress
  • Sass
  • Gulp
  • VH units for fullscreen across devices
  • Modular, Component-based Build
  • Custom JavaScript functionality
  • Fully responsive
